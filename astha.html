About Us

Building financial confidence, one deposit at a time

Astha Sanchay Prokolpo is a grassroots savings organization dedicated to helping rural and urban families in Bangladesh save consistently, access transparent records, and plan for a stronger future. Every taka is tracked, every member is valued.

Safe & Transparent

Every deposit is recorded and visible to the member instantly.

Community First

Run by and for the community, with local branches you can visit.

Steady Growth

Simple monthly saving plans that grow into real financial security.

Our Services

Everything you need to save with confidence

Simple, honest financial services designed for everyday families.

Monthly Savings

Flexible monthly saving plans tailored to your income and goals, with automatic due tracking.

Deposit & Withdrawal

Easy cash, bKash, Nagad and Rocket deposit options with instant digital receipts.

Digital Statements

Download and print your full deposit history and account statement anytime.

Branch Management

Multiple branches connected under one transparent, centrally managed system.

Due Reminders

Automatic reminders for pending monthly deposits so you never miss a payment.

Secure Accounts

Role-based access and password protection to keep every account safe.
